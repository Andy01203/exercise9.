Our school has three different kinds of courses, which are divided into "financial education, postgraduate entrance examination, tutorial class, children's art creativity course, computer application technology, basic general mathematics and international applied English" Six different courses have their own characteristics.           1.Course introduction

1. Financial education: including the education of financial service recipients, consumers and investors, the education of potential financial service recipients, and the popularization of financial knowledge among citizens.

2. Postgraduate entrance examination guidance class: provide guidance for the national postgraduate examination to help students successfully enter the postgraduate examination.

3. Children's art creation course: help children improve their art literacy.

4. Computer application technology: provide teaching assistance to students interested in learning computer and cultivate their computer technology.

5. Basic general mathematics: help students with difficulties in mathematics to improve the foundation, so as to improve the subject performance in the school.

6. International Applied English: help students who have difficulties in English and those who need to go abroad to train their oral English.
